## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Swipe Savvy has developed and owns a proprietary digital fundraising platform known as the "Digital Camp Card" platform, including mobile applications, web portals, payment processing integrations, and merchant management systems (collectively, the "Platform");

WHEREAS, Council desires to utilize the Platform to operate a digital fundraising program enabling Scouts to sell Digital Camp Cards to supporters; and

WHEREAS, the parties wish to establish the terms and conditions under which Council may use the Platform for its fundraising activities.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements hereinafter set forth, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS

**1.1 "Digital Camp Card"** means a digital subscription product delivered via the Platform that grants purchasers access to a curated set of merchant offers, including standard offers and designated one-time-use offers.

**1.2 "Platform"** means the technology infrastructure provided by Swipe Savvy, including but not limited to: mobile applications (iOS and Android), web portals, payment processing systems, merchant management tools, reporting dashboards, and all associated software and services.

**1.3 "Scout-Sold Cards"** means Digital Camp Cards sold directly by Scouts to customers through personal selling efforts, utilizing Scout-specific referral codes or QR codes.

**1.4 "Direct-to-Consumer Sales"** means Digital Camp Cards sold through the Apple App Store, Google Play Store, or the Swipe Savvy website without Scout attribution.

**1.5 "One-Time-Use Offers"** means merchant offers that may only be redeemed once per subscription period and are replenished upon renewal or repurchase.

**1.6 "Program"** means the Digital Camp Card fundraising initiative operated by Council using the Platform.

---

## ARTICLE 2: GRANT OF LICENSE

**2.1 License Grant.** Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, Swipe Savvy hereby grants to Council a non-exclusive, non-transferable, limited license to use the Platform solely for the purpose of operating the Program within Council's geographic territory.

**2.2 Territory.** The license granted herein is limited to Council's chartered geographic territory as defined by the Boy Scouts of America.

**2.3 Reservation of Rights.** All rights not expressly granted herein are reserved by Swipe Savvy. Council acknowledges that Swipe Savvy retains all right, title, and interest in and to the Platform, including all intellectual property rights therein.

**2.4 Restrictions.** Council shall not:
- (a) Sublicense, sell, lease, or otherwise transfer the Platform or any rights therein to any third party;
- (b) Modify, adapt, translate, reverse engineer, decompile, or disassemble any portion of the Platform;
- (c) Use the Platform for any purpose other than the Program;
- (d) Remove or alter any proprietary notices on the Platform.

---

## ARTICLE 3: PRODUCT DESCRIPTION AND PRICING

**3.1 Digital Camp Card Features.** Each Digital Camp Card shall provide purchasers with:
- (a) Access to all standard merchant offers for the subscription period;
- (b) Access to designated one-time-use offers;
- (c) Electronic delivery via mobile application or web access upon purchase.

**3.2 Scout-Sold Digital Camp Cards.**
- (a) **Retail Price:** $10.00 per card
- (b) **Sales Method:** Sold directly by Scouts to customers using personalized referral codes or QR codes
- (c) **Revenue Allocation:** One hundred percent (100%) of gross revenue from Scout-Sold Cards shall be retained by Council for distribution according to Council's internal policies

**3.3 Renewal/Repurchase of Scout-Sold Cards.**
- (a) **Retail Price:** $10.00 per renewal
- (b) Customers may repurchase to regain access to one-time-use offers after redemption
- (c) **Revenue Split:**
  - Swipe Savvy: $5.00 (50%)
  - Council: $5.00 (50%)

**3.4 Direct-to-Consumer Sales – App Stores.**
- (a) **Retail Price:** $15.00 per card
- (b) **Platform Fee:** Fifteen percent (15%) retained by Apple/Google
- (c) **Net Revenue After Fee:** $12.75
- (d) **Revenue Split:**
  - Swipe Savvy: $6.375 (50% of net)
  - Council: $6.375 (50% of net)

**3.5 Direct-to-Consumer Sales – Swipe Savvy Website.**
- (a) **Retail Price:** $15.00 per card
- (b) **Platform Fee:** None
- (c) **Revenue Split:**
  - Swipe Savvy: $7.50 (50%)
  - Council: $7.50 (50%)

## ARTICLE 4: PAYMENT PROCESSING AND REVENUE DISTRIBUTION

**4.1 Payment Processing.** Swipe Savvy shall be responsible for all payment processing through the Platform, utilizing Authorize.net or such other payment processors as Swipe Savvy deems appropriate.

**4.2 Collection of Funds.** All funds from Digital Camp Card sales shall initially be collected by Swipe Savvy through the Platform's payment processing systems.

**4.3 Revenue Reporting.** Swipe Savvy shall provide Council with detailed reporting on a monthly basis, including:
- (a) Total number of cards sold, broken down by sales channel;
- (b) Gross revenue collected by sales channel;
- (c) Applicable platform fees (App Store/Play Store);
- (d) Net revenue calculations;
- (e) Revenue distributions owed to each party;
- (f) Scout-level attribution data for Scout-Sold Cards.

**4.4 Distribution Schedule.** Revenue distributions owed to Council shall be remitted within thirty (30) days following the end of each calendar month, via electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Council.

**4.5 Audit Rights.** Council shall have the right, upon reasonable notice and during normal business hours, to audit Swipe Savvy's records related to Program revenue, not more than once per calendar year.

## ARTICLE 5: ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

**5.1 Swipe Savvy Responsibilities.**
- (a) Provide and maintain the Platform, including mobile applications, web portals, and backend systems;
- (b) Ensure Platform availability with commercially reasonable uptime;
- (c) Process all payments and manage revenue distribution;
- (d) Provide merchant onboarding and offer management services;
- (e) Deliver customer support for Platform functionality issues;
- (f) Provide Scout and Troop Leader training materials;
- (g) Maintain data security in compliance with applicable laws;
- (h) Provide regular software updates and enhancements.

**5.2 Council Responsibilities.**
- (a) Oversee and approve Program implementation within Council territory;
- (b) Recruit and coordinate Scout participation in the Program;
- (c) Promote the Program through Council communication channels;
- (d) Ensure compliance with BSA fundraising policies and guidelines;
- (e) Provide timely approval for brand usage and marketing materials;
- (f) Designate a Program coordinator as primary point of contact;
- (g) Distribute Scout-earned revenue according to Council policies;
- (h) Ensure compliance with COPPA and youth protection requirements.

## ARTICLE 7: DATA PRIVACY AND SECURITY

**7.1 Data Ownership.** Council shall retain ownership of all Scout and customer data collected through the Program. Swipe Savvy shall have a license to use such data solely for Platform operation and Program support.

**7.2 COPPA Compliance.** Both parties shall comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and shall implement appropriate parental consent mechanisms for users under 13 years of age.

**7.3 Data Security.** Swipe Savvy shall implement and maintain commercially reasonable security measures to protect all Program data, including:
- (a) Encryption of data in transit and at rest;
- (b) Access controls and authentication requirements;
- (c) Regular security assessments;
- (d) Incident response procedures.

**7.4 Data Breach Notification.** In the event of a data breach affecting Program data, Swipe Savvy shall notify Council within seventy-two (72) hours of discovery.

## ARTICLE 8: TERM AND TERMINATION

**8.1 Initial Term.** This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of one (1) year (the "Initial Term").

**8.2 Renewal.** This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one (1) year periods (each a "Renewal Term") unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least sixty (60) days prior to the end of the then-current term.

**8.3 Termination for Convenience.** Either party may terminate this Agreement for any reason upon ninety (90) days' prior written notice to the other party.

**8.4 Termination for Cause.** Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other party:
- (a) Materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days of written notice;
- (b) Becomes insolvent, files for bankruptcy, or ceases operations;
- (c) Engages in conduct that materially damages the other party's reputation.

**8.5 Effect of Termination.** Upon termination:
- (a) All license rights granted herein shall immediately cease;
- (b) Swipe Savvy shall remit all outstanding revenue owed to Council within thirty (30) days;
- (c) Swipe Savvy shall provide Council with all Program data in a standard electronic format;
- (d) Active subscriptions shall continue until their expiration, with revenue distributed per this Agreement;
- (e) Each party shall return or destroy confidential information of the other party.
