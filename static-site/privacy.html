1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide when using the App:

Account Information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Profile photo (optional)

Payment Information:

  • Credit/debit card details (processed securely by Authorize.net; we do not store full card numbers)

  • Transaction history

Scout/Troop Information:

When you use the App, we automatically collect certain information through cookies, log files, and similar technologies:

Usage Information:

  • App features accessed
  • Time, date, and duration of use
  • Screens viewed and navigation paths
  • Actions taken within the App

Location Information:

  • GPS coordinates (with your explicit permission)
  • IP address-based approximate location
  • Nearby merchant searches
