Where your support goes

Every Camp Card purchase helps a young Scout experience something that changes their life.

When you buy a Camp Card, you're not just getting discounts — you're sending a Scout to camp.

Your support funds the experiences that build confidence, resilience, and character in young people across Central Florida. Here's what Camp Card makes possible:

  • Summer camp — A week of outdoor adventure, skill-building, and lifelong friendships.
  • Leadership training — Programs that teach Scouts to lead with integrity and serve their communities.
  • Gear and supplies — The equipment Scouts need to explore, learn, and grow safely.
  • Unit activities — Campouts, service projects, and hands-on learning throughout the year.

Many Scout families depend on fundraising to make camp affordable. Your Camp Card purchase closes that gap — and you get great local discounts in return.

Every card makes a difference

Scouting America's Camp Card program has helped thousands of young Scouts across Central Florida attend camp, earn badges, and build skills they carry for life. When you buy a Camp Card, you become part of that story.

Common questions

Everything you need to know before you buy.

What do I get when I buy a Camp Card?

A digital card with exclusive discounts from local merchants — plus the knowledge that your purchase directly supports a Scout's journey.

How do I access my Camp Card?

Download the Camp Card app on iOS or Android. Your digital card and QR code are ready as soon as you sign in.

Where can I use my Camp Card?

At participating local merchants in the Central Florida area. You'll see the full list of offers once you activate your card.

How does my purchase help Scouts?

100% of Camp Card proceeds fund camp experiences, leadership programs, and outdoor adventures for Scouts in your community.

How do I get help?

Visit our Contact Us page or email support@campcardapp.org.
